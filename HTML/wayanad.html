Karappuzha Dam

A perfect starter for a delicious visual treat of Wayanad. Simple but Strong, that makes Karapuzha one of the best sights here. The exuberant water, drenched hills, spectacular environment, and green islands make it a perfect spot for tourists from all around. Placed on the both sides of Karapuzha River, the dam is one of the largest earth dams of India. The best part of everything is that Caffeine Villa is just 12 km away from this eternal beauty.

Kuruvadweep

Kuruva Dweep is a preserved river delta, comprising a bundle of islands over the center of Kabini River in Wayanad. Spread over 950 acres of land, Kuruva Island is heavily occupied with rich flora and fauna. The geographical specifications of this island makes this place evergreen with a peacefully ambience. The famous boat rides on bamboo rafts is one of the most exciting things about Kuruva Island for tourists. 13.3 km away from Caffeine Villa you could dwell deep into this packets of island.

Karlad Lake

The third largest fresh water lake in Kerala. Karlad Lake also hold the title of being the second largest in Wayanad. Ziplining, boating, rock climbing the list of adventure waiting for you out there goes on. There is also a handicrafts and spices emporium. The 7 acres land surrounding this lake is a popular picnic spot. Take a 14.6 km drive from the wonderful Caffeine Villa, you are there...!!!

Pookode Lake

Do you wish to pedal a boat all around India? Make your wish come true at Pookode Lake just 19.3 km away from Caffeine Villa. The wonderful freshwater lake has the shape of Indian map. Nestled between evergreen forest and Western Ghats, the lake is spread over 13 acre and is 40 m deep. Boating in the lake, viewing the eternal beauty of the lake is a unique experience. A visit to this lake is always recommended.

Meenmutty Falls

At a distance of 18.6 km from Caffeine Villa you would reach the the largest and most spectacular waterfall in Wayanad. Gushing down through various layers, Meenmutty Falls downpour from a height of over 800 feet, it is a spectacular fall situated inside thick forest flowing through rocky platform. Meenmutty Falls situated amidst serene location offers wonderful experience to the visitors.

Edakkal Caves

Do you wait for a walk back to the lost past? Your longing comes to an end at Edakkal Caves just 16.6 km away from Caffeine Villa. It is considered to be one of the earliest centres of human habitation. You could witness the extreme beauty of rock carvings within the two natural rock formations believed to have been formed by a large split in a huge rock. Trek up to the historical cave for an everlasting experience.

Ancient Jain Temple

At a distance of 16.6 km from Caffeine Villa, you reach one of the most important and ancient Jain temples in Kerala. The Ancient Jain Temple at Sulthan Bathery dates back to the 13th century. The temple is said to have been built in the prevalent architectural style of the then reigning Vijayanagar dynasty. The doorways of sanctum and mahamandapa have sacred Jain figures. A visit to the Jain Temple is like a trip to spirituality clubbed with a journey to ethnicity.

Banasura Sagar Dam

Banasura Sagar Dam holds the record of being the largest earth dam in India and the second largest in Asia. The Dam is placed in the foothills of Banasura hills,which is the third largest peak in the Western Ghats. A boating trip in the lake and the dam is also a unique experience for the travellers. You could explore this overwhelming beauty just 16.4 km away from Caffeine Villa.

Heritage Museum

At a distance of 14.8 km from Caffeine Villa, you could explore the rare collection of several ancient instruments & tribal artifacts of 14th - 16th century sculptures at The Wayanad Heritage Museum. The museum exhibits fabulous items from tribal communities which dates back to the second century AD. The museum is arranged in two levels with statues occupying lower level while the tribal artifacts displayed at the first level.
